Assalamu Alaikum

Hello, I am Md. Hasan Imam Bijoy. One of the great human creations of Allah. Thank to Allah that Allah created me and sent me to worship for Allah in this beautiful world. The happiest and saddest people in the world. I am not a saint, but if you consider a saint as a sinner who is trying to be honest, then so am I. I am learning from the reality of the way of life. Mix with loving people (but not with extravagant ones). Saying that there is no Seriousness in life, I have suffered a lot of insults, hatches, pains, maybe I have to do more. Maybe Allah did not create me with Seriousness! I like to think of everyone as my own, because I find Muslim brotherhood in everyone. I don't criticize anyone, I just analyze them. I have endless time for the man. Money laundering - I can't look anyone in the eye or talk. Sometimes the desire to be a writer arises, but the desire is lost due to thought. It feels good to walk alone in the afternoon feeling bored. Love to catch fish with a Fishing rod in the afternoon on the river bank or pond side. You can say my bad habit or crazy, I love to wear t-shirts upside down, and always wear upside down!
